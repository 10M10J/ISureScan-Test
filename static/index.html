About Us

We are Stirpot, a company dedicated to using AI and Machine Learning to make your life easier. Our special product, ISureScan, is designed to help you understand your insurance policies better. With ISureScan, you can easily convert complex insurance documents into simple summaries. One great feature of ISureScan is that you can choose the language you prefer, whether it's English or Hindi. This means you can chat with your insurance policy document in the language you're most comfortable with. At Stirpot, we believe in making technology simple and useful for everyone. ISureScan is our way of ensuring that managing your insurance is easy, clear, and personalized to your needs. We are committed to continuously improving our products to meet your needs.
